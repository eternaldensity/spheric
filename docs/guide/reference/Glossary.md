# Glossary

Bureau terminology and game concepts.

---

## General Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Operator** | The player character; a Bureau field agent |
| **The Bureau** | The Federal Bureau of Control; the governing organization |
| **The Board** | A mysterious extradimensional entity; the [[Board Contact Quest|endgame objective]] |
| **The Hiss** | A parasitic [[The Hiss & Corruption|corruption]] that spreads across the sphere |
| **Altered Item** | A paranatural [[Altered Items|anomaly]] on a tile that grants building bonuses |
| **Object of Power** | A permanent [[Objects of Power|bonus]] earned by completing a clearance tier |
| **Clearance Level** | Your [[Research Case Files|research progression]] tier (0–8) |
| **Case File** | A [[Submitting Research|research objective]] requiring specific item submissions |
| **Shift Cycle** | The day/night cycle with 4 phases (Dawn, Zenith, Dusk, Nadir) |
| **Tick** | The game's internal time unit; 1 tick = 200ms |
| **Evolution** | A [[Creatures & Containment|creature]] bonus doubling after 600s of continuous assignment |
