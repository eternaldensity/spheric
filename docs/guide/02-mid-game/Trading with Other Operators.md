# Trading with Other Operators

The **Exchange Terminal** (Trade Terminal), unlocked at [[Reaching Clearance 1|Clearance 1]], enables asynchronous trading between Operators.

## Setting Up

1. Place an Exchange Terminal and connect it to your [[The Conveyor Network|conveyor]] network
2. Route the items you want to trade into the terminal

## Creating a Trade

1. Open the **Exchange Requisitions** panel (**T** key)
2. Click to create a new requisition
3. Specify what you're **offering** and what you **want** in return
4. Your offer becomes visible to all other Operators

## Accepting a Trade

1. Browse **Open Requisitions** in the trading panel
2. Accept an offer that interests you
3. Both players feed the required items into their Exchange Terminals
4. Once both sides are fulfilled, items are available for pickup

## Trade Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open | Waiting for someone to accept |
| Accepted | Both parties working on fulfillment |
| Completed | Items ready for pickup |
| Cancelled | Trade was cancelled |

## Strategy

> [!tip] Specialize and Trade
> - Focus your production on what your territory is rich in
> - If you have abundant Resonance Crystal but no Astral Ore, trade your surplus
> - Late-game items like [[High-Tech Manufacturing|Hypercomputation Cores]] are valuable trade goods
