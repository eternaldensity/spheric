# World Events

After **tick 1000**, the sphere periodically experiences global events every 100–500 ticks. These affect all Operators simultaneously.

## Event Types

### Hiss Surge
**"ALERT: Hiss Surge Active"**

- [[The Hiss & Corruption|Corruption]] spreads faster
- More Hiss entities spawn
- Intensity increases on existing corrupted tiles

> [!warning] Prepare your defenses before the first surge hits. Make sure your Purification Beacons and Defense Arrays are in place.

### Meteor Shower
**"EVENT: Meteor Shower"**

- New **resource deposits** appear on random empty tiles across the sphere
- A chance to find resources closer to your base
- Scout nearby tiles after the event to find new deposits

### Resonance Cascade
**"EVENT: Resonance Cascade"**

- All [[Altered Items|Altered Item]] effects are **temporarily doubled**
- A Resonance Accelerator tile gives 4x speed during the event
- Maximize production during these windows

### Entity Migration
**"EVENT: Entity Migration"**

- [[Creatures & Containment|Creatures]] relocate to new biome-appropriate tiles
- New capture opportunities emerge
- Check your [[Your Starter Kit#Gathering Post|Gathering Post]] area for new arrivals

## Event Notifications

Events are announced at the top of the screen with their Bureau designation. Keep an eye on the notification area to react quickly — especially to Hiss Surges.
